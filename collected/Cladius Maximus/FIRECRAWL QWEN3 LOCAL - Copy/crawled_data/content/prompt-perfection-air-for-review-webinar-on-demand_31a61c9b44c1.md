### Recorded Webinar

AI Advantage: Aiming for Prompt Perfection? Level up with Relativity aiR for Review
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  
The new era of generative AI in the legal world has brought the anticipation of accelerated, higher-quality review. But it's important to craft prompt criteria to set yourself up for success.

With the right prompts your teams can achieve the upmost efficiency and, ultimately, take on higher-value tasks. 

Hear from industry leaders and Relativians in an informative discussion on crafting prompt criteria for aiR for Review, Relativity’s newest generative AI product that helps teams conduct review, prioritize important documents and uncover valuable insights.

Key takeaways:  

*   Tips and tricks for drafting precise prompt criteria
*   Learnings from customers that are experimenting with this new way of interacting with data
*   How to prepare for using aiR for Review for document classification
